STRANGER THINGS

Stranger Things is a thrilling sci-fi series set in the 1980s in the town of Hawkins, Indiana, where mysterious experiments have opened a gateway to a dark dimension called the Upside Down. Follow the adventures of Eleven and her friends as they face supernatural creatures, uncover government secrets, and fight to protect their town.

This fan site, created by Maheen, explores the world of Stranger Things through characters, monsters, Easter eggs, and interactive elements for fans to enjoy.

Timeline of Seasons

Season 1

Will Byers disappears, Eleven escapes Hawkins Lab, and the first gateway to the Upside Down opens.

Season 2

The Mind Flayer begins influencing Will, and new threats emerge as the gate expands.

Season 3

Starcourt Mall becomes the center of danger as the Russians attempt to reopen the gate.

Season 4

Vecna rises, creating portals by targeting victims through their trauma and invading Hawkins.
